Hello!

I'm Igli, a motivated game programmer striving to write clean and efficient code. Having plenty of first hand experience with working in a team, I have grown to be an enthusiastic, hard-working team member, who's passion is the development of story and gameplay driven games!

Over the past 2 years I have been working on several team projects and personal projects. When i am not developing games you will find me learning about javascript ,web-design and programming architecture.

In the free time I enjoy playing video-games and guitar.

I am currently studying Game Engineering at Saxion University Of Applied Sciences Netherlands (2nd year).

I have been programming games since 2016 and have gained experience in critical thinking and problem solving. ​

Projects

A collection of some of my best work

Circle Fly

During the summer of 2018 I challenged myself to find a game idea, develop it, polish it and finally publish it. I decided to make a hyper casual game for mobile devices (IOS/Android). Circle fly was created in 3 weeks and is currently available to download on both Appstore and PlayStore. During the development of this game I got to learn about ad's workflow, menus, In game purchases, tweening.

Planet X

Planet X is a game our team developed during the Game Jam 2019. The team consisted of 3 people 2 Designers and 1 Engineer. Being the only Engineer in the group, I got to learn a lot about unity and how to make a fun game in only 48 hours.

Insectomancer

Insectomancer was developed as a school project during 2nd year of studies. It was developed by a team of 8 people (3 Engineers, 5 Designers) including me.  Working with so many people made me learn more about working in a team using tools such as Git and Sourcetree and methodoligies like Scrum. In this project I got to work on charatcer movement/abilities, companion movement/abilites, Hud, UI and Menus. The game was made using Unity Engine and C#
